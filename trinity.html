Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antonio, Texas. It is a Liberal Arts college that was established in 1869.


Photo courtesy of Trinity University

Trinity has a beautiful 125 acre campus that is filled with nature everywhere you look. As a student here I really love how many trees and plants there are around campus. With 49 majors and 61 minors Trinity offers a range of topics that will suit your intrest.

The features about Trinity that I appreciate most are the sense of community and friendliness that can be felt around the campus. I love how everyone is so nice and is willing to help anyone out.


I chose Trinity University because it is the only school in San Antonio that offers the major that I want, Chinese. I am very excited to be learning about the culture and language of China. I also chose Trinity because of the small school population. With a class size ratio of 9:1, you can really get close to your class mates and professors.
